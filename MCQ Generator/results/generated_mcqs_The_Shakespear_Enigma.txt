## MCQ 1
Question: What was the unusual characteristic of the fruit on the Shakespear tree?
A) They tasted like apples.
B) They grew in the shape of stars.
C) They shimmered with a golden hue.
D) They glowed in the dark.
Correct Answer: C) They shimmered with a golden hue.

## MCQ 2
Question: What happened to Emma after she ate a Shakespear?
A) She forgot her dreams.
B) She gained the ability to fly.
C) She saw visions of her future.
D) She turned into a tree.
Correct Answer: C) She saw visions of her future.
