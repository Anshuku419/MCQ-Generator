## MCQ 1
Question: What was unique about the fruit of the Shakespear tree?
A) It tasted like apples.
B) It grew in unusual shapes.
C) It shimmered with a golden hue.
D) It had no seeds.
Correct Answer: C)

## MCQ 2
Question: According to the legend, what would happen to someone who tasted a Shakespear?
A) They would become invisible.
B) They would gain a glimpse into their future.
C) They would be granted eternal youth.
D) They would understand animal languages.
Correct Answer: B)

## MCQ 3
Question: What did Emma see in her visions after eating the Shakespear?
A) A dark and stormy sea.
B) Herself standing on a grand stage and wandering through enchanted forests.
C) A pot of gold at the end of a rainbow.
D) A map leading to hidden treasure.
Correct Answer: B)
